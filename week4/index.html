The Lyra Hoop

The Lyra, also known as aerial hoop, has an origin dated back to the 19-th Century. It's hard to verify the exact date of origin, but that was when the first recorded performance was documented.

Lyra Fun Facts!

Lyra is a great and fun way to stay in shape. You use all of your muscles and there are tricks and choreography ranging from beginner to intermediate! Such as:

  • Knee Hangs
  • Elbow Hangs
  • Lyra sit ups
  • Neck Hangs
